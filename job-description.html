Company Description

SipSation is a leading beverage company established in 1995, initially as a lemonade stand in Hong Kong. Since then, we've grown into a global brand known for spreading joy and happiness with our flavorful beverages. With a diverse range of products including soft drinks, juices, energy drinks, cocktails, and tonic & mixers, we cater to consumers across 11 countries in Asia and Europe.

Our mission is to design delight and excellence, ensuring every sip exceeds expectations while upholding the highest standards of safety and quality. We generated over USD 40 billion in net revenue in 2023 and are poised for further growth as we expand into new regions.

At SipSation, sustainability is paramount. We're actively reducing our carbon footprint by replacing plastic packaging with biodegradable alternatives and increasing the use of hybrid and electric vehicles. Additionally, we prioritize community engagement by providing economic empowerment opportunities, particularly in Southeast Asia where our manufacturing facilities are located.

Join us at SipSation and be part of a dynamic team dedicated to spreading joy and enhancing lives through innovative and delicious beverages.

Job Title/Employment Type

  • Digital marketing coordinator
  • Full-time
  • Regardless of previous work experience

Job Description

We are looking for a creative and data-driven Digital Marketing Coordinator to assist in the planning, execution, and optimization of our online marketing efforts. The ideal candidate will have a passion for digital marketing, excellent communication skills, a strong understanding of social media platforms, and digital advertising strategies.

Key Duties and Responsibilities

  • Track performance of social media engagements through analysis of key metrics
  • Run regular social promotions and campaigns on social media across the region based on data review
  • Analyzing marketing performance
  • Produce, review, and publish contents
  • Assists with coordination of social media influencer and media visits
  • Assists with presentation materials for sales, marketing and PR meetings
  • Acts as brand ambassador to SipSation at all times
  • Manage all direct messages from social media channels and posts

Qualifications and Skills

  • Bachelor's degree preferably: Marketing, Communications, Digital Media
  • Familiarity with online marketing channels
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Experience in digital media, social media content creation, team management
  • Proficiency in graphic design tools (adobe, etc) and proficiency in social media platforms
  • Comfortable in a fast-paced environment
  • Excellent data analytics and have appropriate insights

Preferred Qualification

  • Understanding of SEO and SEM
  • Proficiency in foreign languages (Cantonese, Korean, Spanish etc)
  • Certifications in digital marketing (Google Analytics, IPA Foundation Certificate etc)

Work Environment

  • Thrive in a dynamic team environment where creativity and collaboration are highly value
  • Expect to work closely with cross-functional teams including marketing, design, and sales to develop and implement digital marketing strategies
  • Our office fosters a supportive atmosphere where ideas are encouraged, and have the opportunity to contribute to the company's growth
  • Much of work will be computer-based

Salary and Benefits

  • $49K + /yr
  • Healthcare coverage: Comprehensive health insurance coverage for employees
  • Paid time off: Generous vacation, holiday, and sick leave benefits to support work-life balance
  • Flexible work arrangements: Flexible work hours and remote work options, allowing for greater flexibility and autonomy
