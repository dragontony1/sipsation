Company Description

SipSation is a leading beverage company established in 1995, initially as a lemonade stand in Hong Kong. Since then, we've grown into a global brand known for spreading joy and happiness with our flavorful beverages. With a diverse range of products including soft drinks, juices, energy drinks, cocktails, and tonic & mixers, we cater to consumers across 11 countries in Asia and Europe.

Our mission is to design delight and excellence, ensuring every sip exceeds expectations while upholding the highest standards of safety and quality. We generated over USD 40 billion in net revenue in 2023 and are poised for further growth as we expand into new regions.

At SipSation, sustainability is paramount. We're actively reducing our carbon footprint by replacing plastic packaging with biodegradable alternatives and increasing the use of hybrid and electric vehicles. Additionally, we prioritize community engagement by providing economic empowerment opportunities, particularly in Southeast Asia where our manufacturing facilities are located.

Join us at SipSation and be part of a dynamic team dedicated to spreading joy and enhancing lives through innovative and delicious beverages.

Digital Marketing Coordinator

We are looking for a creative and data-driven Digital Marketing Coordinator (full-time) to assist in the planning, execution, and optimization of our online marketing efforts. The ideal candidate will have a passion for digital marketing, excellent communication skills, a strong understanding of social media platforms, and digital advertising strategies.

Please submit your resume and cover letter outlining your relevant experience and why you're interested in joining our team for application. Additionally, include any portfolio or work samples that demonstrate your digital marketing skills. Please send your application to HR@SipSation.com with the subject line "Digital Marketing Coordinator Application."

Key Duties and Responsibilities

  • Tracking performance of social media engagements through analysis of key metrics
  • Running regular social promotions and campaigns on social media across the region based on data review
  • Analyzing marketing performance
  • Producing, reviewing, and publishing contents
  • Assisting with coordination of social media influencer and media visits
  • Assisting with presentation materials for sales, marketing and PR meetings
  • Acting as brand ambassador to SipSation at all times
  • Managing all direct messages from social media channels and posts

Work Environment

  • A dynamic environment where creativity and collaboration are highly valued
  • A collaborative environment where you are expected to work cross-functionally with teams including marketing, design, and sales to develop and implement digital marketing strategies
  • A supportive environment where new and creative ideas are encouraged

Qualifications and Skills

  • Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, and/or Digital Media
  • Experience with online marketing channels such as digital media and social media content creation
  • Proficiency in graphic design tools (e.g. Adobe)
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Comfort with fast-paced environments

Preferred Qualifications

  • Understanding of SEO and SEM
  • Proficiency in foreign languages (Cantonese, Korean, Spanish)
  • Certifications in digital marketing (e.g. Google Analytics, IPA Foundation Certificate)
